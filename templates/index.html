About us

We are a group of ambitious and passionate young Mauritians who readily fights against environmental pollution in an unconventional manner. Each one of us, different in culture, background, academic avenues, have a will to pave the way towards a greener and cleaner environment where peace and harmony between human beings and Nature prevails above all. Regardless of all the beautiful things that differentiate us as Humans, we are all selflessly one when helping Mother Nature. Our Main Goal is to reset the relationship between Men and Nature.

What we do?

Sov Lanatir is a registered NGO thriving to make the world a better place. We are a network of youngsters fighting together to create lasting change all across Mauritius and our communities. We aim at preserving our priceless Fauna and Flora and repopulating our endemic plants all around the island. It is an action platform which helps to invigorate people from all across the globe to be more environmentally conscious. Our Team

Sov Lanatir want to induce this behavioral change in us, human beings, by sensitizing people through education, sustainable projects, creative ideas, art, music, etc. We welcome Mauritians especially our youth from all walks of life to contribute to the cause in their own way. From our sports department, a movement of 5 steps Hiking whereby hikers are encouraged to pick up litter as they trail has been created. From our Flora department, we have had numerous clean-ups and planting of endemic plants. From our Arts department, we have helped in reusing plastic water bottles to construct the dragon of Tangloon in China Town. Each department of Sov Lanatir work collaboratively to bring change to this world. These are just a few of the sustainable action we have taken and endorsed to build a better future with the help of our diversified Heads of departments.
